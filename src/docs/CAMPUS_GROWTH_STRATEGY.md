## Campus Growth Model

### The Campus Network Effect Threshold

A campus becomes self-sustaining when > 25–30% of active students are on the platform.
Below this threshold: requires active marketing.
Above this threshold: word-of-mouth drives growth organically.

## Campus Acquisition Funnel

### 5-Step Campus Expansion Model

```
Step 1: Creator Beachhead
  - Recruit 1–2 influential student creators BEFORE launching at that campus
  - Creator posts 3–5 pieces of content about StudentOS to their existing audience
  - Their audience (mostly same campus) joins through creator referral link
  - First 100 users on campus = creator's audience
  - Cost: ₦10k creator grant + ambassador stipend

Step 2: Ambassador Activation
  - Assign 1 ambassador per campus (student with WhatsApp group authority)
  - Ambassador posts invite link in class/department WhatsApp groups
  - Referral code = ambassador's identifier → track ambassador ROI
  - Ambassador goal: 200 signups in first 2 weeks
  - Cost: ₦5,000/month stipend + platform credits

Step 3: Department Penetration
  - Create official departmental groups (CS dept, Law faculty, etc.)
  - Encourage course reps to use StudentOS for announcements
  - Course rep = influence node → reaches entire department
  - Platform creates "Join your department group" onboarding prompt
  - Cost: Zero — product-driven

Step 4: Network Density Event
  - Host 1 digital "campus launch event" per campus (Twitter Space or live session)
  - Top creator from that campus goes live
  - Everyone invited via ambassador WhatsApp distribution
  - Gift animation visible to all viewers → social proof for gifting
  - Cost: ₦10k creator gift sponsorship for event

Step 5: Organic Compounding
  - Once > 15% campus penetration: invite prompt on every profile
  - "3 of your classmates are on StudentOS" (social proof in onboarding)
  - Campus leaderboard creates competitive posting dynamic
  - School course groups → news + study resources → daily return reason
```

## Inter-Campus Virality

### Cross-Campus Content Discovery

Once multiple campuses are on the platform:
- "Trending at UNILAG" section visible to all Lagos-area campuses
- Inter-campus debates in comment threads → discovery of users from other schools
- "Campus vs Campus" challenge format (engineered virality)
- National creator leaderboard → top UNILAG creator becomes known to students at OAU

### Referral Economics

**Campus Referral Program:**
```
Student refers friend from same campus:
  Referrer reward: 200 XP + "Campus Pioneer" badge (at > 5 referrals)
  Referee reward: 100 XP + welcome gift (limited gift from catalog)
  Platform cost: ~₦50 equivalent in wallet credits per successful activation
  
Ambassador referral commission:
  ₦500 per verified active user (active = posted + returned D7)
  Ambassador earning potential: 40 referrals/month = ₦20,000/month
  → Ambassador is incentivized to quality-recruit, not spam
```

**Referral K-Factor Target:**
- Phase 0: K = 0.3 (each user brings 0.3 new users)
- Phase 1: K = 0.6 (improving with product + campus density)
- Phase 2: K = 1.0 (viral — each user brings at least 1 new user)
- Phase 3: K > 1.2 (hypergrowth — each user brings >1.2 users on average)

K > 1.0 means zero paid acquisition needed. Platform grows on its own.

## School Verification & Institutional Partnership

### School Verification Process

```
Level 1 — Community Verified (auto):
  Trigger: > 100 users self-identify as attending [school]
  Effect: School appears in "popular campuses" list
  No institutional involvement required

Level 2 — Platform Verified (manual):
  Trigger: School admin submits verification request
  Process: Platform confirms school existence via official website check
  Effect: Blue school badge, official school group created
  Cost to school: Free

Level 3 — Institutional Partner (paid):
  Trigger: School signs Standard plan (₦150k+/year)
  Process: School provides official student email domain (e.g., @unilag.edu.ng)
  Effect: Email-verified enrollment, school-branded interface, analytics dashboard
  Value to school: Engagement data, announcement tools, official presence

Level 4 — Enterprise Partner:
  Trigger: School signs Enterprise plan (₦500k+/year)
  Effect: Full LMS integration, SSO, gradebook access, school API
```

### Student Email Verification (Phase 2 feature)

Students with `.edu.ng` email addresses get:
- "Verified Student" badge (gold checkmark variant)
- Trust score boost (+10 points)
- KYC fast-track (verified email = identity confirmed for wallet purposes)
- Unlocks higher gift sending limits

Value to platform: verified enrollment = premium audience for advertisers.

## Department-Level Expansion Strategy

### Why Departments (not just schools)

Campus network effects are stronger at department level:
- 150 CS students know each other → any one of them joining creates FOMO for others
- Department WhatsApp groups = distribution channels
- Course reps = micro-influencers (every student knows the course rep)

### Department Penetration Tactics

1. **Department Groups (product):** Pre-create groups for top 5 departments at each campus
2. **Course Rep Partnership:** Identify course reps, give them "Course Rep" badge, incentivize posting announcements
3. **Exam Season Activation:** "Past questions" and "study resources" distributed through department groups → highest-value reason to join during exam period
4. **Department Leaderboard:** "Top students in Computer Science at UNILAG" → competitive posting
